Privacy Policy for ARSA

Revision 1.0

01-24-2024

1. Introduction

ARSA is an application designed for sentiment analysis of local public-facing government pages in the State of Arkansas. Our goal is to provide insights into public sentiment for the benefit of citizens and state officials. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and handling any personal information responsibly.

2. Information Collection

ARSA collects data through the Facebook API. This data is used exclusively for the purpose of generating sentiment analysis. We do not store personal information of any users permanently. The data collected is limited to that which is necessary for conducting accurate sentiment analysis.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is processed to generate sentiment analysis results. These results are then provided to citizens and officials of the State of Arkansas to offer insights into public opinion on various matters.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

No personal data is shared with any third parties. Only the results of the sentiment analysis are provided to the intended recipients, which include state officials and citizens of Arkansas.

5. User Rights

As ARSA does not retain personal user data, users maintain full rights over their data as per Facebook’s policies. Our application’s use of information received from Facebook APIs will adhere to the Facebook Platform Policy.

6. Data Security

Since ARSA does not store user data, the focus of our data security is on ensuring the integrity and security of the sentiment analysis results.

7. Data Retention

User data is not retained in our system; it is only used transiently to generate sentiment analysis.

8. Children’s Privacy

ARSA does not specifically target or collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to the Policy

Any revisions to our privacy policy will be published here, with an incremented revision number and the date of modification. Users are encouraged to review our policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact the Facebook App developer through the app’s contact features.
